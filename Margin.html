Margin refers to the space around an element, outside of any defined borders. It creates space between elements. The CSS properties used to control margin are similar to padding: margin-top: Specifies the margin on the top side of the element. margin-right: Specifies the margin on the right side of the element. margin-bottom: Specifies the margin on the bottom side of the element. margin-left: Specifies the margin on the left side of the element.
